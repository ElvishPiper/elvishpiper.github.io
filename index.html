About Darrell

On A Journey To Create Something Incredible

As a kid like any other, games were the gateway from the mundane days of homework; I was captivated by its ability to evoke strong emotions, which kickstarted my journey into game design and coding in general.


Although having started as a software developer, life has led me through many other fulfilling areas of work, and with many more stories to tell. A love of the Japanese language and cars also brought me to Japan where I resided for 8 years. Along the way, I've picked up a couple other skills, but I've never stopped trying to create things that can inspire.
